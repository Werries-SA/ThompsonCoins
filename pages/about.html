Our Story

Thompson Coins was founded in 1975 by Jonathan Thompson, a passionate numismatist with a vision to preserve and share the rich history of coins with collectors and enthusiasts worldwide.

What began as a small collection in Johannesburg has grown into one of South Africa's premier numismatic galleries, featuring rare and valuable coins from ancient civilizations to modern minting marvels.

Today, the Thompson family continues this legacy, combining decades of expertise with a commitment to authenticity and education in the fascinating world of coins.

Our Expertise

Authentication

Every coin in our collection undergoes rigorous authentication processes by our team of experts, ensuring provenance and legitimacy.

Research

Our dedicated research team documents the historical context and significance of each coin, providing comprehensive information to collectors.

Valuation

We offer professional appraisal services, helping collectors understand the true value of their numismatic treasures.

Conservation

We employ industry-best practices for coin preservation, ensuring these historical artifacts remain pristine for future generations.
